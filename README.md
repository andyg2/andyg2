## 💫 About Me
Freelance developer since the mid-90s. Emigrated from the UK to a small island in the Philippines in 2008 and have been building from there ever since - often through typhoons, brownouts, and the occasional fried UPS.

- 🔭 Currently building **RAMP** - a retail analytics platform processing data from cameras and sensors globally, built on a minimalist PHP / Nginx / ClickHouse / MariaDB stack. Also working on **Bartons Lens**, a UK property analytics platform, and **MCI**, an accounting and ordering system for a high-end shop fitting company in Melbourne.
- 🧠 I like building fast systems without frameworks. ClickHouse pipelines, Docker-based harvesters, MQTT tooling, license-plate and object-detection pipelines, PWAs for low-connectivity environments.
- 💬 Ask me about **PHP at scale, ClickHouse, computer vision pipelines, performance optimization, prototyping, data flow, and keeping production alive in places the power isn't.**
- 🧪 Honest about AI tools: genuinely useful for coding, but I watch closely for silent logic errors and the gap between "appears to work" and "actually understood."
- 📫 Reach me at **<andy@dgte.pro>**
